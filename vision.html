Vision and Mission

INSTITUTE













          Motto

"Nurturing aspirants supporting growth"

         Vision

"Acharya Institute of Technology, committed to the cause of sustainable valuebased education in all disciplines, envisions itself as a global fountainhead of innovative human enterprise, with inspirational initiatives for Academic Excellence".

         Mission

"Acharya Institute of Technology strives to provide excellent academic ambiance to the students for achieving global standards of technical education, foster intellectual and personal development, meaningful research and ethical service to sustainable societal needs".

ECE DEPARTMENT













         vision

"To be a premier engineering department with excellence in teaching, research and innovation to meet the global industrial standards and to have significant impact on the well-being of the society".

         Mission

To provide student centric learning environment, inculcate profound knowledge in both fundamental and applied areas of science and technology.

To train and mentor the students in developing leadership qualities and team building skills along with professional ethics.
